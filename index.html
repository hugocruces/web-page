About

I am an economist specializing in quantitative analysis and economic modelling. Currently I work at the European Commission's Joint Research Centre, where I am part of the Fiscal Policy Analysis Unit.

I lead a team analysing how public healthcare systems contribute to fairness and social resilience in the EU. I also develop EUROMOD, a microsimulation model used by European institutions to assess the distributional and budgetary impact of tax and benefit policies. My work has directly supported policy making in several EU Member States.
