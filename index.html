Hugo Cruces | Economist

About

I am an economist working in the Fiscal Policy Analysis Unit of the European Commission's Joint Research Centre.

I lead a team analysing the contribution of public healthcare systems to fairness and social resilience in the EU and develop the microsimulation model EUROMOD, which is used by European Institutions to study the distributional impact of tax-benefit policies. My work has supported policy making in several Member States.
